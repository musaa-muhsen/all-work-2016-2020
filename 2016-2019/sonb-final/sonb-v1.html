Sounds of Nature's Bird's is a website containing a small collection of exotic bird sounds taken in the wild. When the user interacts with the play button a ten second clip is played and information of the bird is next to it. The reason for creating this site is my passion for nature and also for clean fresh design, which I tried to combine the two on this project. For the aesthetics I took inspiration from black and white posters and music art work.
